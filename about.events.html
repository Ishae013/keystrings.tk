About KS Music Events
        KeyStrings started performing in the wedding scene before the school started. They are one of those unnamed group who performs during weddings of their friends. After numerous wedding gigs, they have decided to adapt the school’s name as the name of their ensemble. They are a mix of musicians from different conservatories and some from different bands and ensembles as well. Their diversity is not a hindrance in creating memorable music for your special occasion.

Booking Process
Step 1: Send an SMS/EMAIL/CALL KeyStrings regarding your desired package

Step 2: Once rate is finalized, kindly fill out the client information sheet attached on the contract given.

Step 3: Pay the down payment 30% of the total package or PHP1000 reservation fee

Step 4: Receive the signed contract with acknowledgment receipt. Balance can be paid on the day of the event
