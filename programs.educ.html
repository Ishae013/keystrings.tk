Learn. Play. Create
     We value your learning through our Individual and Group class programs. We created a curriculum in such a way that it is attainable and at the same time enjoyable for every learner, young or young-at-heart. At the end of the 12 lessons, our goal is for every student to learn the basic on their chosen instrument or they should have attained the level they desired to accomplish.
      Our vision doesn't end in learning and playing instruments. We also value your creativity in order to expand your musicianship. We encourage our students to create their own music, or even compose melodies and songs. Learning music is not just about playing your favorite Classical or popular tunes. Our goal is to unleash the creativity in every person.
Individual Lessons
     This class is designed for students who wants to study a particular instrument and would like to excel through one-on- tne learning. Each level can be achieved according to the pace of the student. Normally, a student finishes a level after the 12-lesson period. For this class, its duration is one hour per lesson. Lessons can be conducted once, twice, or thrice a week (depending on the need of the student). Here are our rates and program for individual classes:

  • We recommend PIANO, VOICE, VIOLIN, and GUITAR*
  • Thrice or twice a week is recommended for maximum retention of skill
  • 5500PHP for 12 lessons, one hour per lesson
    • JUNIORS (Ages 8 to 12 years old)
  • All courses are offered: PIANO, VOICE, VIOLIN, GUITAR, DRUMS
  • - Once or twice a week lesson is recommended
  • - 5500PHP for 12 lessons, one hour per lesson
    • TEENS & ADULTS (Ages 13 years old and above)
  • All courses are offered: PIANO, VOICE, VIOLIN, GUITAR, DRUMS
  • Once a week lesson is recommended
  • 5500PHP for 12 lessons, one hour per lesson
Group Class
     Ever wonder if your kids really want to play the instrument or they just want to try it? Worry no more! KeyStrings designed a basic music class that is really affordable and will let your kids try an instrument in a class. Introducing, the BASIC MUSIC CLASS. This class is designed especially for kids who just wants to try and learn a particular instrument, just for the sake of exposing the child to music. Each course class is divided into 3 sections according to age group [Pre-School, Juniors, and Teens & Adults]. Only 5 students is allowed per class (according to age group as well).
      We normally offer this class every summer. Please do check our facebook page for more details.
Ensemble Class
     This class is FREE for all aspiring musicians who would like to have an avenue to rehearse ensemble pieces arranged by KeyStrings. Although this ensemble class is not yet available (due to schedule conflicts), but we do believe that ensemble playing can enhance a person's musicality. We'll keep you posted on this program.
