About KS Music Education
Learn Play Create
Our goal for our students is to learn their desired instruments and enhance their musicality. Besides instrument learning, we also give them music theory lessons incorporated in their instrument class. After learning, we encourage our students to join the recitals and play their instrument. Also, we want them to perform in a group as well. We believe that they can also learn more by performing with other musicians. Music learning doesn't end in playing your favorite Classical or popular tunes. It is a beginning of creating your own music. We, at KeyStrings envisions our students to create their own music or song.
        Liza Coroneza
